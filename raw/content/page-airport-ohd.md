## Airport Overview

**Ohrid St. Paul the Apostle Airport (OHD)**
Gateway to Lake Ohrid and the southwestern Macedonia region — a key seasonal tourism hub with year-round scheduled operations.

AAM has operated at OHD since 1997, originally as General Sales Agent for Swissair and Swiss International. Today we provide ground handling supervision and passenger services for seasonal charters and scheduled carriers operating into Ohrid.
