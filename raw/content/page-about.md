## Who We Are

AAM DOOEL Skopje provides station management, ground handling supervision and full aviation support services at Skopje International Airport (SKP) and Ohrid St. Paul the Apostle Airport (OHD).

We act as the local representative for international airlines operating in Macedonia — supervising third-party ground handlers, coordinating flight operations, and managing irregularity response on the ground.

Our deep local knowledge, 30+ years of operational experience and extensive regional network allow us to respond promptly to client needs — which is why airlines use us for ground handling supervision at both Macedonian international airports.

## Our History

### 1992–1995 — Founded
AAM was established in 1992 with mixed ownership (51% Adria Airways, 49% domestic capital). Flight operations began in September 1995 at Skopje Airport with Adria Airways as the founding airline partner.

### 1997–2006 — Swissair & Swiss International
AAM became General Sales Agent for Swissair — and later Swiss International following the airline's restructuring — providing full airport supervision at both SKP and OHD.

### 1997–2025 — IATA & Amadeus Training
AAM operated an IATA training center for tariffs and ticketing, and an Amadeus GDS training center — building the aviation expertise base in Macedonia.

### 2006 — Fully Independent
Following the end of mixed ownership, AAM became a 100% privately owned Macedonian company — the structure it maintains today.

### 2008–present — Regional Expansion
AAM began supporting JETEX (Dubai) across Macedonia, Albania, Serbia, Montenegro and Slovenia — expanding beyond the two airports into a regional operational network.

### 2010–present — Edelweiss Air
Representation and supervision agreement for Edelweiss Air at SKP, OHD and Albania. One of our longest-running current partnerships.

### 2015–present — Air Serbia
Supervision and ticketing agreement for Air Serbia at both SKP and OHD.

### 2018–present — LOT Polish Airlines
Ticketing agreement for LOT Polish Airlines covering Macedonia.

### 2019–present — Flight Pro International
Airport support agreement for Flight Pro International (Houston, USA).

### 2023–present — Turkish Airlines
Ticketing agreement for Turkish Airlines covering Macedonia.

## AAM Group

AAM DOOEL Skopje is the parent company of a group of aviation-focused businesses:

**Avionet Travel Agency DOO Skopje** (avio.net.mk)
Macedonia's only IATA/BSP-accredited consolidator. Supports 40+ non-IATA travel agencies across Macedonia with consolidator fares, BSP ticketing and back-office services. Also provides corporate travel management.

**AAM Airport Services DOO**
Part of the AAM Group, providing complementary airport services.
