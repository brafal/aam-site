## Airport Overview

**Skopje International Airport (SKP)**
Alexander the Great Airport — Macedonia's primary international gateway.

AAM maintains a permanent operational presence at SKP — acting as local representative for international airlines, supervising third-party ground handlers, and managing all aspects of station operations.

---

## What AAM Provides at SKP

### Station Management
- Permanent station representation & supervision
- Flight operations assistance
- Irregularity handling & response
- Liaison with port authorities and airport management

### Passenger Services
- Dedicated ticket sales desk — Arrivals Hall (permanent)
- Check-in & gate supervision
- Arrival & transfer services
- VIP & special passenger handling
- Special assistance services
- Document & visa check at gate

### Ground Handling Supervision
- Full ramp supervision on behalf of the airline
- Coordination with TAV (ground handler at SKP)
- Baggage sorting oversight & transportation
- Turnaround coordination & compliance
- De/Anti-icing, GPU & push-back
- ULD control & load supervision

### Fuel & Logistics
- Fuel arrangements with negotiated supplier rates
- Crew hotel accommodation (HOTAC)
- Crew transportation & transfers
- Catering coordination
- Cargo coordination & support
