## Service 03 — Ground Handling Supervision

**Headline:** Ground Handling Supervision
**Subtext:** We supervise the handlers, so you don't have to.

Full ramp handling supervision at SKP & OHD. AAM coordinates third-party ground handlers (TAV) on behalf of the airline — ensuring safe, compliant and on-time turnarounds on every flight.

**What's included:**
- Ramp supervision & turnaround coordination
- Baggage sorting oversight & transportation
- Cabin cleaning & crew transport
- De/Anti-icing, GPU & push-back coordination
- ULD control & load supervision
